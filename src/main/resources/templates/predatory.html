Predatory fish are those kind of fish species that hunts or ambushes other animals for food. These can be fish that bite chunks out of their prey; such as members of the piranha family or consume them whole; such as the red-tailed catfish. Predatory fish are distinct from scavenging species which, while they will consume other fish species, they will do so more opportunistically, feeding upon already dead fish or the remains of someone else’s meal.
